About Braeside Bowling Club

A bit of history and what we're all about.

Started in 1965, Still Going Strong

A group of local bowlers got together and raised the money to build the first green. Added the second one in '72 and the third in '85. The clubhouse's been done up a few times since — most recently in 2022.

We've always been a proper community club. Not fancy, not pretentious. Just people who enjoy a game of bowls and each other's company.

Our Story

Key Moments

1965
Club founded. First green opened with 48 founding members.
1972
Second green built. Membership tops 100 for the first time.
1985
Third green added. A Team wins first county cup.
1998
Major clubhouse renovation. New bar and function room.
2010
Floodlights installed on all three greens.
2022
Clubhouse fully refurbished. Green 2 completely relaid.
2025
A Team win the League Cup. Membership at 156 and growing.
